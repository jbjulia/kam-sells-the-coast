FAQWe Have Answers

  • What's my housing budget?
  • As a general rule, you want to spend no more than 30 percent of your monthly gross income on housing.
  • How much should I save for a down payment?
  • You should aim to put 20 percent down on your home, but no less than 10 percent.
  • How much are closing costs?

  • You'll likely pay around 2 - 5 percent of your home's purchase price in closing costs.

  • Do I need to save for moving expenses?
  • Unless your paying your friend with pizza to help you move, don't let these expenses catch you by surprise. Start preparing now.
  • Are there any problems with the house?
  • The answer to this question isn't always easy to find. You must get a home inspection before you buy.
  • How old is the roof?
  • Most expert roofing websites say that an asphalt roof has a lifespan of around 20 years.
  • How old are the appliances?
  • Don't forget to ask about the life expectancy of household appliances, including HVAC and water heaters.
  • What are similar homes selling for?
  • This is a great question for your realtor. Answering this question can help you see if the home you want to buy is set at a fair price.
  • What's the reason for selling?
  • See if you can find out why the seller is leaving. This may help you at the negotiation table.
